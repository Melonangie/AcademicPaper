# State-of-the-Art Research Survey in Deep Learning

## Objective

To explore the current state and future trends in the rapidly evolving field of Deep Learning

## Instructions

Your submission should be in the form of a research survey paper, following some IEEE double-column template, for example [this one](https://www.ieee.org/conferences/publishing/templates.html).  The maximum page limit is 5 pages, excluding references. The paper should be submitted as a PDF file.

Select a sub-field or specific area in Deep Learning that interests you the most. This could range from specific architectures to applications or challenges.
    
Your paper should include a comprehensive literature review. Use databases like Web of Science, Scopus, Google Scholar, arXiv,  Elsevier, and IEEE Xplore to find papers. You should have a minimum of 7 references, and all of them of them should be from the last three years. Older references may be included but will not count within the 7 required references.
    
Do not merely summarize the papers. Instead, offer a critical analysis. Discuss the significance of the work, its impact, and limitations.
    
Identify gaps in the current state of the research and suggest possible future directions.

Your paper should be well-structured, comprising of the following sections:
    
- Abstract
- Introduction
- Literature Review
    - This section should include a critical analysis or the surveyed worked, i.e. not just a summary
    - Avoid presenting the papers as bullet points, the literature review should present a cohesive story.    
- Future Directions
- Conclusion
- References

Ensure that all sources are correctly cited, following the IEEE, APA, Vancouver or other accepted citation style.

You may have help from generative tools such as ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, etc., however, this should be used responsibly, and the work should be your own.

## Grading criteria

- Depth and breadth of literature review
- Quality of critical analysis
- Clarity and structure of the paper
- Relevance and quality of references

| Criteria| Description| Ratings |
| :-------| :----:| ------------:|
| Critical analysis of literature | The paper not only sumarizes the presented works, but also analyses possible areas of future work, impact of the research, relevance, and other insight. | 50 pts |
| Quality of written language| The work does not contain typos or spelling mistakes. | 10 pts |
| References | The work contains at least 7 references. | 20 pts |
| Correct citations | All the references are cited correctly, using APA, IEEE or other accepted format. | 20 pts |
